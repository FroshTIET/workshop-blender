FROSH2020

3D Modelling And Its Applications

September 17th to 19th 2020 ABOUT:

The students will be taught the basics of blender including modelling, lighting and rendering a complete scene in blender. We will also teach them how to use those models to create their own Instagram filters. Blender is a very versatile tool and the skills taught in this workshop can easily be used in a variety of different fields including game development and animation.

*It is advised to use a mouse.
*All the students are required to download blender version 2.8 or greater.
